Ultimate Smile Design

A beautiful smile can do wonders for a person’s self-esteem and confidence.

Smile design is the process of creating a custom smile that is both aesthetically pleasing and functional. The goal of smile design is to enhance the patient’s overall appearance and create a smile that is natural looking and fits the individual’s facial features.

There are many factors that need to be considered when designing a smile, such as the shape and size of the teeth, the color of the teeth, the spacing of the teeth, and the alignment of the teeth. Here is where ADVANCE DENTAL EXPORT steps in.

Traditionally, getting smiling designing done has been a very tedious task. Right from taking a manual impression from the patient’s mouth to courier the impression, doing manual wax-ups, manual build-up and sending back the impression has been an extremely time consuming and impractical process. However, through modern technology, Digital dentistry is revolutionising the way we design and create beautiful smiles.

Why Ultimate Smile Designing?

Smile designing with Advance Dental Export is done using digital dentistry. As the name suggests all the important processes are digital. We perform digitised X-rays, take a primary impression and make temporary template ready to be transferred in the patient’s mouth. All the necessary changes are made and the final template is ready for the patient.

However, we make sure we don’t lose sight of what the most important aspect of this procedure is, which is the aesthetic. The final template is given the artistic touch manually to ensure the patient receives an all-round end result.

While working on a particular case we take into consideration all the aspects which are necessary for smile designing. These can vary depending on the individual’s needs and desires. First, the shape and size of the teeth are considered. Second, the color of the teeth is important, as it can be used to brighten or whiten the smile. Third, the alignment of the teeth must be taken into account, as crooked or gapped teeth can be corrected with orthodontic treatment or veneers. Fourth, the gum tissue be healthy and free of any disease or inflammation. the overall facial proportions and symmetry is considered to ensure that the smile is in harmony with the rest of the face. Through this we are able to provide a custom made, personalised end result to each and every patient with little to no scope of errors.
